What Is Vision?

It is an Artificial Intelligence Model created by the Logical Order which aims to identify common traffic signs, especially Russian ones. It can prove to be very useful in techologies such autonomous and semi-autonomous cars and even traffic cameras.

Aim

Throughout the world, self driving cars are gaining more and more importance and are being seen as a viable techology for the future. As driving accidents are a major cause for deaths, the development of self-driving cars will be crucial in saving countless lives. But in order to do so, they need to ensure that they can correctly recognize traffic signs and thus, we made this software to aid them in this crucial goal.
